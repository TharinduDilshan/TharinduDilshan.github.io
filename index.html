About Me

I'm Tharindu Sooriyaarachchi and I am from Galle.

I completed a B.Eng. (Hons) Software Engineering Degree in Informatics Institute of Technology Sri Lanka (affiliated with University of Westminster UK) and I worked as a Software Engineering Intern at CAM Management Solutions (CAMMS) for one year.

I have worked in many projects. I did my final year project which was deep learning approach to identify tea leaf diseases in Sri Lanka with image processing.

I did my second year group project which was an autamated air conditioner system and I did my first year project which was baby montioring system using IOT.
